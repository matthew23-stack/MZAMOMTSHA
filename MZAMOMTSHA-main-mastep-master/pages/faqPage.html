Frequently Asked Questions

Where can I buy branded school uniform.
You can buy school uniform from the school. Please visit the school for information on uniform and prices.
What do I do when I need to see a teacher.
Staff can be contacted via phone calls, e-mails or SMSs. Credentials will be provided according to your child's classes. An appointment can be made via the school office.
What happens when my child has an accident at school.
All Teaching Assistants are first aid trained and can offer reassurance and first aid to pupils who are injured. If injury is significant, we would inform parents and appropriate first aid would be given and of course any serious injuries occurrence would resort to immediate medical service involvement.
What do I do if my child has prescribed medication.
If your child is prescribed medication and needs to have it at school, you must complete a form at the school office. All medication held on school premises is kept locked and secure.
How my child be able to contribute their views.
We have several ways that children's views can be heard and listened to, including: School Council Pupil Voice Interview Class Council Meeting etc. Conform to the school for more information.
