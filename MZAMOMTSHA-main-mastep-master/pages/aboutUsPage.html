About Us

The Mzamomtsha Primay School was established in 2008 in driftsand city of cape town.The school was initially created for primary school students from grade 1 to grade 7, but due to the rapid population growth the school is also enrolling higher grades.Students are allowed up to grade 12 - which is the metric year.The Mzamomtsha Primary School attracts and produces some of the country’s best students minds, making it the first choice for many individuals seeking to pursue a high level education in the township location. Housed within our higest grades major subject such as: Physical Science, Pure Maths, Accounting and Life Science.

The Principle

MR Simphiwe Khuze was born on the 13th of March 1972. He was born and raised in Middleburg, Eastern Cape. He studied his primary years at Mchobololo Primary School in the Eastern Cape and served his high school years at Archie Velile Senior Secondary where his passion for teaching begin.

Teaching Staff

The School’s teaching philosophy is underpinned by a strong commitment to exposing students to both the theoretical and practical aspects of learning, and our lectured teaching is complemented by teaching students in their home languages. Facilitated in smaller groups, classes are highly interactive and students are given a unique opportunity to understand in depth and put in practise the theory to which they have been exposed to in lectures.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to create a safe atmosphere in which effective learning and teaching can take place. We provide physical and recruit the best human resources to compliment learning. We engage in staff development programmes that will set our staff apart as good role models of the profession. We collaborate with parents, the community and the business sector in order to support the learning programmes, and we teach our leaners values that will make them complete citizens.

OUR VISION

At Mzamomtsha Primary School we are committed to empower every learner in our school to be innovative, impactful and independent inidividuals who will grow academically and contribute positively to the upliftment of their community to take up a meaningful and productive position in society.
